FRESHMEN ORIENTATION CAMP
30 June - 3 July
Hey! Trying to get used to university life? Join us at our Freshmen Orientation Camp for lots of fun and exciting adventures! Forge new friendships and enjoy the many activities that we have planned for you! During this camp, close-knitted friendships are sure to bud between the incoming freshmen of SoC! Come and join us to experience Beach Day, Mystery Night and many more!
At FOC, freshmen will play a series of fun and interesting station games around the campus. Here, our freshmen are our own version of soccer sitting on chairs! Our committee has tried and tested every single game to ensure that they will be really fun for you! Come this year to find out what we have in store for you!
During Sports Day, freshmen will get exposed to all the Inter-Faculty Games (IFG) sports to encourage them to play for IFG representing our School of Computing! Don’t miss this opportunity to try out sports that you’ve always wanted to try but was too afraid of trying!
You can never have a camp without an awesome Beach Day! We have spent countless hours into planning the perfect games so that freshmen will get to enjoy themselves to the fullest!
End off FOC with a blast!
Here's what our seniors say about FOC!

Really glad to have been given the opportunity to be part of something bigger like FSC. Honestly went in with zero expectations as it was my first camp. Same usual awkward introductions at the start but it somehow led to lasting friendships till now. FSC has opened up many opportunities to me such as RAG and that was where I made my first group of friends in NUS. These are the bunch where you chill and banter with and also will be the people you see throughout your first semester and more to come. Simon Lam, Y1 Computer Science

Going to FSC was one of the best choice i made in my uni life. It was so much fun and I made so many friends whom I know will stay with me for a long time. It has really added so much colours and excitement into my uni life and who says uni’s all about studying when you get to attend such a camp like this one? Lim Jun Hup, Y1 Computer Science

To be honest, I had no clue what my course or what uni would be like during the super long break that I had before uni started. It was only during FOW where I learnt more about uni life and how to make my life easier in uni! :) My OGLs certainly gave me good advice and stuff so FOP camps are certainly camps which you shouldn't miss!! Foo Yong Cheng, Y1 Info Systems

I’ve not regretted signing up for FSC. Never expected to still be in touch with my orientation group mates, in fact, they were they ones who stuck by me the most and helped me through the past semester. And guess what, I will be rooming with one of my FSC OG mates next semester! Jordan Seow, Y1 Business Analytics
